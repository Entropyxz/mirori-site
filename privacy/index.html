Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026
The short version: Mirori does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. Everything stays on your device. We have no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. That's it.

Data Collection

Mirori does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. Your local seeds, Reflection Studio writing, generated fractal artifacts, and app settings are stored exclusively on your device using local storage.

What We Don't Do

  • We do not create user accounts
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs
  • We do not transmit your seeds, writing, artifacts, or personal information to any server
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies or web beacons

In-App Purchases

Mirori offers a one-time in-app purchase processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play. These transactions are handled entirely by the platform store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is the platform's in-app purchase system (Apple StoreKit / Google Play Billing). No other third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks are included in the app.

Children's Privacy

Mirori does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data.

Data Retention & Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can delete all app data at any time through the app's Settings screen ("Reset Profile") or by uninstalling the app. We retain nothing because we receive nothing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Significant changes will also be noted in app update release notes.
